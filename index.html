Let's re-imagine how democracy gets done.

Flux is a new political party that will act as your gateway to participate directly in parliament. When a bill is put forward you can vote in real time. Flux is here to help redistribute political power and empower the Australian people. Politics will never change unless WE change the system!

ABOUT FLUX / WHY

Why Australia needs Flux

Australian politics is broken. Traditional politicians say one thing to get elected and then do another. Horse trading and backroom deals have become more important than good policy. Flux is here to help upgrade our democracy, pioneering a real-time voting system which gives you a direct voice into parliament. #takethepowerback

Find out more

We're fed up with the political process too

Feel unrepresented? Fed up with broken promises? Vote to change the system. Flux is a new system, not about ideology, but of progress for process. Elect Flux and make your voice heard. Help us contest the 2016 federal election and change ‪#‎AusPol‬ forever.
